nowAround is your all-in-one web and mobile application designed to help you effortlessly explore and engage with Slovak establishments.

Choose your location

Easily select your current location or any area of interest to see what's happening nearby. With NowAround, you're always in the know about the latest events and activities around you.

Pick a place of interest

Browse through a variety of establishments like restaurants, bars, cafes, theaters, and more. Find daily menus, event schedules, and special activities tailored to your preferences.

Enjoy your time

Share exciting events with friends, plan group outings, and make the most of your day. With NowAround, all your plans are organized in one place, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience.
